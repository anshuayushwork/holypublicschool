Admission Procedure (Session 2025-26)

Our Philosophy

We believe in a transparent and fair admission process. Our aim is to identify students who can benefit from our educational environment and contribute to our school community. All admissions are based on merit and the availability of seats.

Eligibility Criteria

The required age for admission to different classes as of April 1, 2025, is as follows:

Class Age Requirement
Play Group 2.5 to 3.5 years
Nursery 3.5 to 4.5 years
Prep 4.5 to 5.5 years
Class I 5.5 to 6.5 years
Class II onwards Based on the Transfer Certificate from the previous school.

Admission Process: Step-by-Step

  1. Step 1: Online Registration
    Fill out the online registration form, upload the required documents, and pay the non-refundable registration fee through our portal. Start Registration Now
  2. Step 2: Interaction & Assessment
    For Play Group to Class I, a simple, informal interaction with the child and parents will be scheduled. For Class II and above, a written proficiency assessment will be conducted. Dates will be communicated via email.
  3. Step 3: Result Declaration
    The list of selected candidates will be published on the school website and displayed on the school notice board. Parents will also be notified via email.
  4. Step 4: Final Admission
    Selected candidates must complete all admission formalities, including submission of original documents and payment of fees, within the stipulated time to secure their seat.
